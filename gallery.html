📁 How to add real photos to this gallery
1
Upload photos to your Google Drive folder QZCS Website/gallery/album-name/
2
Right-click the cover photo → Share → Anyone with the link → Copy link. The link looks like: https://drive.google.com/file/d/FILE_ID/view
3
Convert to a direct image link by replacing the end: https://drive.google.com/uc?export=view&id=FILE_ID
4
In gallery.html, replace the placeholder div with an <img> tag using the direct link, and add all photo direct links to data-photos.
5
Once all albums have real photos, delete this note from the HTML.
