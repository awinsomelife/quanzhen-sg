Join Us

Whether you are drawn to the practice, the community, or the cultural heritage of Taoism — there is a place for you at Quan Zhen Cultural Society. Join as a member or volunteer with us.

Two ways to get involved

Membership and volunteering

會員與義工

入會
Become a member

Membership gives you full access to our community, classes, rituals, and resources. As a member of Quan Zhen Cultural Society, you become part of a living Taoist lineage community in Singapore.

Access to weekly Sunday scripture classes
Priority registration for rituals and ceremonies
Access to learning resources and study materials
Invitations to members-only events and gatherings
Be part of a registered Quanzhen Longmen community
Apply for membership
義工
Volunteer with us

Our volunteers are the backbone of everything we do — from setting up for rituals to supporting events, managing social media, and helping new members feel welcome. No experience needed, just willingness.

Support rituals and ceremonial events
Event setup, logistics, and coordination
Photography, videography, and social media
Administrative and community support
Teaching assistant roles for scripture classes
Register as volunteer

How membership works

1
Submit enquiry

Fill in the membership enquiry form below — tell us a little about yourself and your interest in Taoism.

2
We get in touch

A committee member will reach out via WhatsApp or email within a few days to answer any questions.

3
Come for a visit

We invite you to join a Sunday class or an upcoming event — so you can experience the community before committing.

4
Complete registration

Once you're ready, complete your formal membership registration and payment to become an official member.
