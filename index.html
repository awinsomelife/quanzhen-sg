Our lineage

The Quanzhen Longmen tradition

全真龍門道統

Rooted in one of Taoism's great scholastic lineages, Quan Zhen Cultural Society carries forward a tradition of self-cultivation, communal ritual, and textual study that has endured for centuries. The Quanzhen Longmen school traces its heritage through Wang Chongyang and the Seven Perfected, emphasising inner alchemy, scriptural learning, and ethical conduct.
