全真龍門道統

Rooted in one of Taoism's great scholastic lineages, Quan Zhen Cultural Society carries forward a tradition of self-cultivation, communal ritual, and textual study that has endured for centuries. The Quanzhen Longmen school traces its heritage through Wang Chongyang and the Seven Perfected, emphasising inner alchemy, scriptural learning, and ethical conduct. 全真教化協會植根於道教偉大的學術道統之一，傳承自我修煉、集體科儀及典籍研習的千年傳統。全真龍門道統溯源至王重陽及全真七子，著重內丹修煉、經典學習及道德修養。
